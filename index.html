TerraDestined

Welcome to Melcadia

Melcadia is a land of lush forests and quaint villages, towering mountains and vast seas, deep caves and remote islands. Unfortunately, an evil force has spread over the land, threatening to take over the entire world! Someone needs to do something in order to save this beautiful land and its inhabitants.

Meet Tara

A young woman with the world on her shoulders, Tara has been called to put a stop to the evil force spreading across Melcadia. Though the odds may seem stacked against her, she's ready to rise to the challenge and cleanse Melcadia from the evil one. Armed with her faith and the support of her family and community, anything is possible!

Explore Vast Landscapes

Melcadia features a vast and diverse landscape consisting of deep caves, towering mountains, dense forests, and more! The evil force has caused the creatures that inhabit these landscapes to become aggressive, so Tara will need to tread cautiously and defend herself. Tara can begin to cleanse the land by defeating bosses that live in the deepest, darkest corners of these areas, but it won't be easy!

Brave Harsh Climates

Some of the climates of Melcadia can be harsh and unforgiving. Tara will need to make sure she's prepared with the proper equipment to handle these climates, or her adventure might end sooner than she'd like.

Rely on Family and Friends

Tara will need to rely on the support of her family and community in order to succeed in her adventure. She hasn't spent much time away from home before, so she'll need to remember that while saving the world is important, so is checking in with family.
